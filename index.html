About Us

WE PROVIDE YOUR PET A HAPPY LIFE WITHOUT DISEASES.

We are an animal hospital located in Kottawa. Our Clinic is served by three professionally qualified veterinary practitioners. We provide all types of services such as geriatric and pediatric care, behavioral and nutritional pet clinic counseling, vaccination, treatments and surgeries and advising for health and 24h emergency mobile service.
